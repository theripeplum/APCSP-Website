My partners and I decided to make an interactive two player game. In this game, Spongebob and Patrick are able to shoot fireballs. This code detects for when fireballs, which are turtles, and Patrick/Spongebob, which are also turtles, are within close proximity. If they are, the corresponding character loses a life. The first person to lose all of their lives loses the game.
